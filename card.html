Chinchilla

A nice pet that likes to bite the cables and it can jump very high

  • Scientific Name: Chinchilla lanigera
  • Average Length: 22 - 38cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: Currently in the wild it lives in Reserva Nacional Las Chinchillas in Chile

It has a long mustaches and tail with stiff hair. Its length is 22 - 38cm and weight is 370 - 490g. An animal with the most precious fur in the world. Chinchillas live an average of 10 years but when they live in good conditions, they can live up to 20 years.
